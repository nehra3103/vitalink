How Vitalink can help

Vitalink is an advanced blockchain and IoT-integrated platform designed to ensure the seamless, secure, and efficient distribution of essential medications. By leveraging cutting-edge technologies such as ESP32, MAX30100, and AD8232 for real-time health monitoring, and Hyperledger blockchain for data security, Vitalink enhances transparency, traceability, and compliance in pharmaceutical supply chains.
